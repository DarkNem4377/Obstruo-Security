POLICIES

Our commitment to your privacy, safety, and legal compliance.

PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is our top priority. Obstruo keeps your data safe and private:

  • Minimal data collection: We do not track, monitor, or sell your browsing activity.
  • Local logs only: Limited protection and system activity logs are stored locally on your device.
  • No third-party sharing: Your data is never shared, transmitted, or sold.
  • User control: You can view or delete logs at any time.

Information We Collect

Obstruo collects only minimal information required for core operation. This includes locally stored application events such as startup/shutdown times and protection status changes. We do not collect browsing history, personal information, or identifiable user data.

How We Use Your Information

Local logs are used solely for diagnostic and transparency purposes. All logs are automatically deleted after 24 hours and are never transmitted to external servers.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device and protected using secure hashing and operating-system-level encryption. Authentication PINs are never stored in plaintext.

Your Rights

You have full control over locally stored data. Logs can be reviewed, manually deleted, or completely removed by uninstalling the application.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

CHILD SAFETY

Obstruo is designed to support safer internet use for families:

  • Default safe mode: Adult and harmful domains are blocked by default.
  • Parental controls: Protection settings are secured using a PIN.
  • Supervised use: Intended for parent- or guardian-managed environments.
  • Family-focused: Designed to support households with children of varying ages.

Our Commitment

Obstruo is built to help parents and guardians reduce exposure to inappropriate online content. It functions as a support tool and does not replace active supervision or parental guidance.

Built-in Protection

Obstruo uses curated blocklists of known adult and harmful domains. While effective, blocklists may not capture all content.

Parental Control Features

Configuration changes require PIN verification. Protection state changes are recorded locally for transparency.
