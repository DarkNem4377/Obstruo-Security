POLICIES

Our commitment to your privacy, safety, and legal compliance.

PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is our top priority. Obstruo keeps your data safe and private:

  • Minimal data collection: We do not track or sell your activity.
  • Local logs only: Information about blocked sites is stored locally on your device.
  • No third-party sharing: Your data is never shared with anyone.
  • User control: You can view, export, or delete logs anytime.

Information We Collect

Obstruo collects minimal information necessary for operation. This includes application logs (startup/shutdown times, protection status changes) stored locally on your device. We do not collect browsing history, personal information, or any data that could identify you.

How We Use Your Information

Local logs are used solely for diagnostic purposes to help troubleshoot issues. They are automatically deleted after 7 days and never transmitted to external servers.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption. Your PIN is hashed using SHA-256 cryptographic hashing, making it impossible to retrieve even with direct file access.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data. You can view logs through the settings panel, manually delete them at any time, or completely uninstall the application to remove all stored information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Last Updated: December 2025

CHILD SAFETY

Obstruo is designed to keep children safe online:

  • Default safe mode: Adult content and harmful sites blocked by default.
  • Parental controls: Manage access with a secure PIN.
  • Educational support: Optional guidance encourages safe browsing habits.
  • Age-appropriate: Designed for families with children of all ages.

Our Commitment

Obstruo is committed to creating a safer online environment for children. Our application is designed as a tool for parents and guardians to help protect children from inappropriate content.

Built-in Protection

Our curated blocklist includes thousands of known adult websites, gambling sites, and other potentially harmful content. This list is regularly updated to provide comprehensive protection.

Parental Control Features

Parents can set a secure PIN to prevent unauthorized changes to protection settings. Temporary overrides require PIN verification, and all protection changes are logged for review.

Limitations and Responsibility

While Obstruo provides robust filtering, no technical solution can guarantee 100% protection. We encourage parents to maintain open communication with children about online safety and to supervise internet usage appropriate to the child's age.

Educational Resources

We recommend combining Obstruo with parental guidance and age-appropriate conversations about responsible internet use.
